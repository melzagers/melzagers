Gear used in my photos & videos

This is a small list of the different equipments I use in my photography and videos. This equipment is all bought by me and my girlfriend Ellen. With are own money.

Camera

Panasonic GX80

First of all: I always shoot my pictures in RAW format. The Panasonic DMC GX80 is the camera I use. It can shoot in RAW JPEG. It supports 4K/30fps and 1080p/60fps. Lens we use the standaard Kit lens. 16-32mm lens from Panasonic.

Drone

DJI Mavic Pro

The DJI Mavic Pro is the drone we use for aerial shots and footage. It can shoot in RAW JPEG. It supports 4K/30fps and 1080p/60fps. It can fly up to 67kmph. Range of 7km. 27min flighttime per battery.

Polaroid

Fujifilm instax 8

The fujifilm instax is the polaroid I use. I dont use it for social media but if I want to capture raw moments just as they are.

Action Cam

GoPro Hero4

The GoPro is used for underwater photography or footage.
